Celebrating the 19th National Congress, playing the Guangxi notes of the main theme of "Win-Win Cooperation"

Bring into play the unique advantages of Guangxi and ASEAN countries, accelerate the open development of the Beibu Gulf Economic Zone and the Pearl River-Xijiang Economic Belt, build an international channel for ASEAN, and create a new strategic fulcrum for the opening and development of the southwestern central and southern regions, forming a 21st century maritime silk. The important gateway between the road and the Silk Road Economic Belt. If such a pattern can be formed, Guangxi will develop this game of chess.

1. Only go all out, the mission is not insulting -General Secretary Xi Jinping participated in the Third Session of the 12th National People's Congress and gave Guangxi the "Three Major Positions" New Mission during the Guangxi delegation's deliberation

On September 28 this year, the China-Singapore Southbound Interchange Sea-rail Intermodal Normal Train (Guangxi Beibu Gulf Port-Chongqing) achieved two-way separation for the first time. One day later, the Lancang Railway, which was completed in 9 years, was operated across the board. The train from Gansu officially joined the sea-rail combined transport artery.

This not only indicates the official opening of the Sino-Singapore interconnection southward channel, but also declares that Guangxi, which has the most convenient seaport in western China, has organically linked the “Belt and Road” and implemented the “three major positionings” to take another solid step.

As an important part of China-Singapore's third intergovernmental cooperation project, the Sino-Singapore (Chongqing) Strategic Interconnection Demonstration Project, the southbound channel uses various modes of transportation such as railway, highway, water transport, and aviation. Through Guiyang and Nanning, it will reach the main logistics nodes of Singapore and ASEAN through the coastal ports along the coast of Guangxi, and connect the Central European and Central European routes to Central Asia and Europe to form the first north-south freight passage and the complete international land and sea. The trade circle has achieved a seamless connection between the Silk Road Economic Belt, the 21st Century Maritime Silk Road and the Yangtze River Economic Belt.

2. Spreading the wings for Dapeng -Forming an efficient and convenient transportation network connecting Shangxiang, Xiongtong, Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao, connecting “Shuitiegong” and docking ASEAN

How to practice the "three major positioning", especially the construction of an important gateway for the "One Belt, One Road" organic connection, as the only province in China adjacent to the ASEAN land and sea, has been looking for effective hands.

For a long time, most of the goods in western China have gone through the east-west passage to the sea, and they have traveled to most of China's coastline to reach Southeast Asia. Compared with the Yangtze River shipping channel from Shanghai to Singapore, the southbound channel is shortened by about 2,100 kilometers, the transportation time is reduced by about 15 days, the railway two-way freight rate is reduced by 30%, and the port operation rate is reduced by 50%. The irresistible charm highlights the huge market value and potential.

Therefore, when the South-South channel initiative was launched, Guangxi was keenly seized the opportunity and took the initiative to go to the relevant state ministries and commissions to negotiate and negotiate with the brother provinces. It was strongly supported and promoted by the Chinese and New Zealand countries and regions, and only for more than half a year. The southward channel that has been hailed as the "international spiritual canal" has turned from a blueprint to a reality.

If the "Belt and Road" is likened to the two wings of the Asian take-off, then the interconnection is the bloodline of the two wings. At the 2014 APEC meeting in Beijing, General Secretary Xi Jinping had such a metaphor.

High-speed county and county, high-speed railway city, civil aviation film, and inland rivers and rivers - for Dapeng to spread the wings and dredge the meridian, these years Guangxi accelerated the outbound sea out of the provincial channel construction, focus on the formation of Shanglian Xiaoxiang It is connected to Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao, connecting “Water and Water Public” and connecting to ASEAN's efficient and convenient transportation network.

From the "road network end" to the "regional hub", the Guangxi Railway has achieved a gorgeous turn. Today, Guangxi high-speed railway accounts for about 10% of the country's total mileage. Since 2013, the railway passenger traffic volume has been ranked first in the country. 12 of the 14 districts and cities have opened high-speed trains, initially forming a center with Nanning. "12310" high-speed rail economic circle.

"The further improvement of the Guangxi high-speed rail network not only facilitates the domestic people, but also greatly promotes the tourism cooperation between China and ASEAN." Ma Hansong, director of the passenger transportation department of the Nanning Railway Bureau, told reporters that tourists from Vietnam and other ASEAN countries entered the country from Guangxi. After that, you can take a train to travel to any province around Guangxi.

In order to better play the important role of the organic link "One Belt, One Road", Guangxi adheres to the "two legs" walk: one is land, and strives to open the high-speed railway from Guiyang to Chongqing, Chengdu, Xi'an, Lanzhou and Urumqi to the north. The construction of the large-scale land passage of China Unicom's Central South Peninsula provides transportation support for the construction of the China-Indochina Economic Cooperation Corridor; one is the sea route, focusing on the construction of the Beibu Gulf Regional International Shipping Center, the Regional International Trade Logistics Financial Cooperation Center, and the Port City Cooperation Network. Smooth trade with countries along the Maritime Silk Road.

To build an international channel, not only the southeast and northwest will be "communicated" in all directions, but also all kinds of transportation networks in the sea and land. Guangxi is actively promoting the construction of a three-dimensional transportation system between Jianghai, Tiehai and Luhang, and opening up “capillary vessels” to achieve seamless connection.
