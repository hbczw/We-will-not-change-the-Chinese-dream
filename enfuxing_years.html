Chinese diplomatic failure in Paris, May 4, 1919 in beijing to young students and the masses of our people, citizens, business people and other people in middle and co - participation, through demonstrations, petitions, strikes, and violence against the local government to be able to take various patriotic zeal of Chinese people is completely opposed imperialism,Feudalism in the patriotic movement.1919 directly influenced the birth and development of the Communist Party of China (CPC), CPC party generally define it as" the anti - imperialist and patriotic movement "(Note the" feudal "word is a generalization of feudalism), and movement as the old democratic revolution and the new - democratic revolution of the watershed.
