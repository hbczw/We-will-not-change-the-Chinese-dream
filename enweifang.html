Reconstruction of dangerous houses in rural areas

Dangerous house renovation policy

According to the relevant policies, the transformation of rural dilapidated houses should implement the principle of “three best two”. The "three most" priority is to help some of the most dangerous and economically poorest households in housing to solve the most basic housing security problems. "Two on" generally refers to the transformation method of local reconstruction and reconstruction. Since the end of 2008, the renovation of dilapidated buildings has been the government's project to benefit the people. At the same time, the central government and the government have also set up tasks to support the 2.66 million poor farmers in the country to transform dangerous houses. According to the rural dilapidated housing reform policy, the subsidy targets for dilapidated housing renovation are the five-guarantee households, low-income households and some poor disabled families and other poor households who are scattered in rural areas.

Reconstruction of dangerous houses in Guangxi rural areas

Data show that in 2017, Guangxi exceeded the task of rebuilding dangerous houses in rural areas. The state has issued 4 households in Guangxi rural areas with 71 thousand and 400 key households. Guangxi poverty alleviation dynamics

From 2013 to 2017, Guangxi completed 905,000 rural dilapidated houses and solved the need for safe housing for more than 3.6 million rural poor. During the "Thirteenth Five-Year Plan" period, Guangxi's annual dilapidated housing renovation will focus on completing the poverty alleviation and precision poverty alleviation and construction of the dangerous houses of the poverty-stricken households, so that the people can live in a new farmhouse that is satisfactory, assured and safe.

Case number one

Mengrenqiu is a villager in Beilin Village, Xiyan Town, Shanglin County, Guangxi. Two years ago, a family of Mengrenqiu was still crowded in two dilapidated mud houses. The lighting and ventilation were extremely poor. Due to decades of wind and rain erosion, mud The wall has been cracked and deformed, and it has become a dangerous house. When it is windy and rainy, the Mengren ball working outside is stunned and worried about the safety of the family. In 2015, the village committee informed Mengrenqiu that his house was assessed as a dangerous house and met the conditions for the renovation of dilapidated houses in rural areas. He could apply for the renovation of subsidy funds for the construction of new houses. After receiving the notice, Mengren Ball rushed back to Shanglin's hometown from the working place in Guangdong overnight, filled out the application form according to the procedure, submitted the relevant certification materials, and handled the simple procedures. After that, the village committee of Beilin Village where Mengrenqiu is located convened a village representative to convene a democratic council to discuss all the applications for the renovation of dangerous buildings that meet the conditions for crisis, and then publicized the listed objects and gave relevant materials. The township government will review and finally approve the county. After the approval of the layers, Mengren’s long-awaited house officially started construction.
