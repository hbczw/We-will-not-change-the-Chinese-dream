the establishment of a socialist market economy

In 1992 when Deng proposed the establishment of socialist market economy system reform, the general secretary of the formal establishment of a socialist market economy.In the system of socialist market - oriented economy, market economy and socialism are opposed to each other in a non - traditional.It not only has theory and reality of socialist has had a huge impact, but also to recognize the relationship between the socialist market economy and provide new theoretical thinking.

The asian financial crisis?

On July 2, 1997, the Asian crisis in Thailand, the Thai baht was devalued.Soon, the storm sweeping across Malaysia, Singapore, Japan and South Korea, China, etc.Broke the Asian economy booming.Some of Asia's economic recession started, some of the country also began to unravel.In the Asian financial crisis, China is under great pressure, not to devalue the yuan.Because of its prudent financial policy and previous years has taken a series of risk - prevention measures in the midst of the crisis is not of direct impact on finance and the economy continued to stabilize.

flood victory

In the summer of 1998 basin wide severe floods on that Yangtze, Nenjiang and Songhua rivers burst 300 - year return period flood, the beginning of July, eight times, Jianghan Plain, the Yangtze River at tens of millions of human lives and the entire middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River are facing a great threat.Aug. 13, President Jiang Zemin came to the forefront of the flood embankment.Disaster again shows the great superiority of socialism with Chinese characteristics, the display of the Chinese nation to great cohesion.Chinese troops and civilians to compose of the flood - combating spirit enriched the great rejuvenation of the spiritual resources.

the communist Party of China (CPC)

1997 take place from 12 to 18 in Beijing, this is in China's reform and opening up and socialist modernization, the development of a critical juncture, and march unswervingly and triumphantly along the correct line adopted since the Third Plenary Session of the General Assembly.the 15th National Congress of the Communist Party of China (CPC) said China's reform and construction of new experience, Deng Xiaoping Theory as the guiding ideology of the Party, the rule is determined to be the basic strategy of ruling the adherence to public ownership is dominant and different economic sectors develop side by side,Hold to distribution according to work remains the predominant mode and coexists with various other, determines our country stage in entrylevel in socialism basic economic system and distribution system.The 15th National Congress of the Party and the socialist cause with Chinese characteristics should be further encouraged.

SOE reform

SOE reform: since 1998 to the textile, coal and other key industries and state - owned large and medium deficit - ridden enterprises in the implementation of sweeping reform has achieved significant effect.State - owned enterprises are weeded out outdated capacity, downsizing is completed, a three - year target of extricating China's enterprises were basically realized, and the profitability is greatly improved.

World Trade Organization (WTO)

November 11, 2001, China formally joined the World Trade Organization (WTO), becoming its 143rd member.This is China's modernization in the historical significance of the event, Chinese economic development and social progress in the important and far - reaching influence, indicates that Chinese undertaking opening to the outside world has entered a new stage of development.
