Accurate poverty alleviation

Guangxi regards poverty alleviation and development as the first livelihood project. The experience of Guangxi's accurate identification of the establishment of the standing card was introduced as a typical introduction in the national establishment of the card promotion meeting. At the end of 2015, the cumulative poverty reduction in Guangxi in the past five years reached 5.6 million, exceeding the total population of the district by 1/10, and the incidence of poverty dropped sharply from 23.9% to 10.5%.

"Clean" starts, "ecological" relay, leap to "livable", sprint "happiness"... two years big step, eight dreams can be round. "Three major positioning" opens a new pattern

In the past five years, Guangxi deepened cooperation with countries along the “Belt and Road” with the focus of ASEAN, and strived to build an upgraded China-ASEAN Expo and China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit to form a “Nanning Channel”.
