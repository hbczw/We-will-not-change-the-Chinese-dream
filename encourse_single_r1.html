Retrospect and Prospect of the 15th Anniversary of China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit

On October 8, 2003, Premier Wen Jiabao initiated the China-ASEAN Expo and the China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit at the 7th China-ASEAN Leaders' Meeting of 10+1. It was widely welcomed and warmly welcomed by the leaders of the 10 ASEAN countries. Respond and write a presidential statement. In November 2004, the first China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit (hereinafter referred to as the “Peace”) was successfully held in Nanning, Guangxi. Since then, the "Grand Summit" has been permanently settled in Nanning, Guangxi. The “Symposium” was hosted by the Ministry of Commerce of China, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and the People's Government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. The ASEAN Secretariat and the ASEAN 10 National Chamber of Commerce and Industry co-organized and hosted the China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit Secretariat.

Since its inception in 2004, the “Fly Summit” has been working with China and ASEAN for a total of 15 years. The “Festival” has continued to grow and develop, and has experienced three stages: pragmatic promotion, stable development, and transformation and upgrading.

The first stage: the pragmatic promotion stage. From 2004 to 2010, the main task of the Summit was to promote the signing of agreements such as the Agreement on Trade in Goods, the Agreement on Dispute Settlement Mechanism and the China-ASEAN Free Trade Area Investment Agreement, and promote trade and investment between China and ASEAN business communities. Cooperation.

The second stage: a steady development stage. From 2010 to October 2017, the main task of the “Fly Summit” is to focus on the China-ASEAN Free Trade Area Outlook, Interconnection, Industrial Cooperation, Maritime Silk Road, Ocean Cooperation, Tourism Cooperation, China-ASEAN Destiny Community, etc. With exchanges, we will promote economic and trade cooperation between China and ASEAN from a higher level and a higher level.

The third stage: the transformation and upgrading stage. Since the 19th National Party Congress in October 2017, the summit has entered a new era of transformation and upgrading.

In the past 15 years, the "Fly Summit" has adhered to the principle of promoting comprehensive economic cooperation between China and ASEAN and promoting the construction of China-ASEAN Free Trade Area, actively building a platform for China and ASEAN governments to promote economic and trade policies, and promoting understanding and cooperation between China and ASEAN business community. It promotes a wider interaction and dialogue between the government, academia and the business community, expresses the will of the business community to the government, and becomes a bright business card for cooperation between China and ASEAN, and has achieved six landmark achievements.

1. To build a "megaphone" for political and business dialogue with the symbol of building a China-ASEAN destiny community, and political achievements continue to show

In the past 15 years, 50 leaders of China and ASEAN countries have attended the opening ceremony and delivered speeches. More than 1,500 senior government officials attended the summit and held more than 200 business and economic activities, more than 50,000 of the world's top 500 and China's top 500. Enterprise executives, experts and scholars from ASEAN countries attended the meeting. Put forward positive suggestions on the construction of China-ASEAN Free Trade Area, deepening the strategic partnership between the two sides, deepening two-way trade and investment cooperation, promoting interconnection and interconnection, promoting social and cultural exchanges, promoting sub-regional cooperation, trade facilitation measures, and relaxing non-tariff barriers. It has pointed out the direction for cooperation between China and ASEAN. The round-table dialogue between the leaders of ASEAN countries and the CEOs of Chinese enterprises under the framework of the “Symposium” has been successfully held nine times since its inception in 2009. Each time, one leader of the ASEAN countries and the CEO of a Chinese company promoting bilateral economic and trade cooperation between China and ASEAN are invited. And their partners in ASEAN countries have initiated dialogues, which have led Chinese companies to reach a number of major cooperation projects in ASEAN countries such as Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, Myanmar, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, etc., and promote bilateral economic and trade cooperation projects of more than 40 billion US dollars. In the fields of finance, energy, infrastructure, engineering, logistics, science and technology, education, agriculture, and tourism, we will effectively promote the implementation of major cooperation projects between China and ASEAN countries. Heads of government, senior government officials, business leaders, and entrepreneurs spoke on the same stage, exchanged ideas, the business community clearly understood the government's policies, and leaders also understood the needs of the business community. The "peaks" became the promotion of policy communication and environmental improvement in relevant countries. An effective way to help political dialogue and create a lasting prosperity for the China-ASEAN economy. The "Session" has become a gathering of political leaders from China and ASEAN countries, and merchants and scholars have gathered together. It has become the international conference with the highest and most influential business and investment fields between China and ASEAN countries, and has become a high-level industrial and commercial event in China and ASEAN countries. .

2. To serve the China-ASEAN Free Trade Area as a symbol, to create a “multiplier” for economic and trade cooperation, and to continuously demonstrate economic and trade achievements.

In the past 15 years, the "Feng Summit" has played a unique, crucial and important role in closely following the pragmatic cooperation between China and ASEAN, serving the country's diplomatic strategy, and promoting the open development of Guangxi. It has made important importance for the rapid development of China-ASEAN bilateral trade and investment. contribution. In 2004, the China-ASEAN Free Trade Area early harvest plan was implemented, the "Goods Trade Agreement" and the "Dispute Settlement Mechanism Agreement" were signed. In 2009, the "China-ASEAN Free Trade Area Investment Agreement" was signed. In 2010, China-ASEAN Freedom The trade zone was officially launched in full. In 2015, the upgraded version of the Free Trade Zone was officially signed. The trade volume between China and ASEAN jumped from 55.2 billion U.S. dollars 15 years ago to 514.8 billion U.S. dollars in 2017. Since 2009, China has maintained ASEAN's largest trading partner status for eight consecutive years. ASEAN has been China's third largest trading partner since 2011. The cumulative investment of both parties increased from 32.5 billion US dollars in 2003 to 200 billion US dollars in 2017, an increase of more than five times. The “Grand Summit” settled in Nanning, Guangxi, which not only promoted economic and trade exchanges and regional cooperation between China and ASEAN countries, but also promoted the accelerated development of Guangxi's economy. In 2003, before the summit, the total import and export volume of Guangxi was 3.19 billion US dollars, and the trade volume with ASEAN was 739 million US dollars. In 2017, the total import and export volume of Guangxi was 386.634 billion yuan, and the trade volume with ASEAN was 189.385 billion yuan. It occupies half of Guangxi's import and export volume. The growth of processing trade has accelerated the process of industrialization in Guangxi. In 2003, the total industrial output value of the above-scale industries was only 141.329 billion yuan, and reached 2.6 trillion yuan in 2017.

3. With the establishment of the China-ASEAN Commodity Exchange Center as a symbol, to create an “accelerator” for the southward opening, the opening results continue to show

In the past 15 years, the "Festival" has served Guangxi to open up a new pattern of opening up to the outside world and achieve full coverage of the national strategy. At the opening ceremony of the third "Sum Summit" in 2006, Premier Wen Jiabao put forward the proposal of "actively exploring the feasibility of carrying out Pan-Beibu Gulf economic cooperation." Thanks to the “Peak Summit”, in January 2008, the State Council approved the implementation of the “Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone Development Plan”; in May, it approved the establishment of the Qinzhou Bonded Port Area in Guangxi; in December, it approved the establishment of Guangxi Pingxiang Comprehensive Bonded Zone, Guangxi Beibu Gulf The opening up of the economic zone has risen to a national strategy. Putting "Building China-ASEAN Commodity Exchange Center in Nanning" into General Secretary Xi Jinping and Premier Li Keqiang's keynote speech at the "Symposium" to promote the establishment of China-ASEAN Commodity Exchange Center in Nanning, highlighting Nanning's trade and logistics with China and ASEAN The central position. Promote the leaders of our country to emphasize the continued strengthening of the construction of the China-Malaysia Qinzhou Industrial Park at the opening ceremony of the "Symposium". Actively promote the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the ASEAN Chamber of Commerce and Industry to jointly build a China-ASEAN cross-border e-commerce platform. The southwestern central and southern regions are important hinterland for the development of Guangxi. The “Symposium” has continuously enhanced the function of serving the open and southwestern central and southern regions, and has played a powerful bridge and radiation role. In recent years, the advantages of the “sea” of the Beibu Gulf Port of Fangchenggang, Qinzhou Port and Beigang Port of the coastal area have been accelerated. In 2017, the cargo throughput of Beibu Gulf Port exceeded 219 million tons, and the container throughput exceeded 2,278,700 TEUs. It can be used with more than 100 countries and regions in the world and more than 200 ports, initially forming “big traffic” and “big logistics”. The pattern. The Beibu Gulf Port route has basically achieved full coverage in Southeast Asia, forming a large-scale maritime channel in the hinterland to Southeast Asia, the Indian Ocean, the Pacific Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea. A large number of goods from Guangdong, Shandong and Jiangsu were exported to ASEAN through Guangxi ports, accounting for about 45% of the goods exported by Guangxi to ASEAN countries. Fruit imported from Guangxi Port to China accounts for 50% of the total amount of fruit imported from China. The import volume of Pingxiang mahogany furniture ranks first in the country for 7 consecutive years, becoming the largest import port and display distribution center for mahogany furniture in the country.

4. To promote the “Nanning Channel” of China-ASEAN cooperation as a symbol, to create a “booster” for international cooperation, and the brand achievements continue to show

In the past 15 years, as a major international cooperation mechanism among regions, the “Session” has insisted on strengthening industrial and commercial cooperation within the region, and has achieved fruitful results in the functions of trade and investment promotion, cross-border e-commerce, mechanism construction, and commercial legal dispute resolution services. The Chinese government has listed the "Symposium" with the Boao Forum for Asia and the Davos Forum as "key foreign-related forums and exhibitions held at the national level". It has been held annually since 2014. For the past 10 years, a Politburo Standing Committee has been held every year to form A fixed high-level dialogue mechanism. The China-ASEAN business community issued the "Nanning Declaration" and "China-ASEAN Business and Industry Joint Action Plan on Accelerating Mutual Benefit Cooperation", "China-ASEAN Legal Cooperation Joint Declaration" and "China-ASEAN Commercial Joint Mediation Cooperation Agreement". Through the “Symposium”, the liaison office of the China-ASEAN Business Council and the Liaison Office of the China Council of the East Asia Business Council were established in Nanning, providing a new platform for Guangxi to carry out foreign cooperation. In the past 15 years, the role of the “Gen Summit” mechanism has been continuously expanded. The scope of services has been extended from the original “10+1” (ASEAN 10 plus China) and “10+3” (ASEAN 10 plus China, Korea, Japan) to 2005. “10+6” (10 countries in ASEAN plus China, South Korea, Japan, Australia, New Zealand, India), and in 2013, extended to countries along the “Belt and Road” such as Kazakhstan.

5. With the promotion of the China-ASEAN Information Port as a symbol, we will create a “server” for industrial upgrading, and the industrial achievements will continue to show

In the past 15 years, the "Fly Summit" has been committed to promoting coordination and cooperation mechanisms in the fields of business, transportation, customs, quality inspection, finance, science and technology, environmental protection, mining, humanities, etc., attracting entrepreneurs, experts and scholars from China-ASEAN countries to gather in Guangxi. We will carry out various forms of economic and trade activities around the optimization of industrial structure, and promote the transformation and upgrading of Guangxi's industries from the region to the international, from the single to the plural, from the primary to the high quality. China-ASEAN Finance, Mining, Telecommunications, Logistics, E-commerce, Law, Port, Agriculture, Manufacturing, Tourism, Energy, Transportation and other dozens of forums and exhibitions organized by the Summit have spawned the China-ASEAN Information Port. Accelerated the development of China-ASEAN communications industry, promoted the construction of China-ASEAN port city cooperation network, promoted the construction of Nanning regional international financial center, promoted the China-ASEAN Ministerial Mining Conference mechanism, and established China-ASEAN logistics The Intermodal Alliance has established a China-ASEAN cross-border e-commerce platform. A total of more than 100 cooperative projects have been promoted, and the amount of cooperation has reached more than 40 billion yuan, which has promoted the integration and development of Guangxi's primary, secondary and tertiary industries.

The number of two-way tourists increased from 3.87 million in 2003 to nearly 50 million in 2017. The “Grand Summit” brought huge traffic and material flow.
