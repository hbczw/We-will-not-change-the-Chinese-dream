Cultural soul

"The cultural movements are in line with the national movement, and the context is connected with the national pulse."

Thoughts and values are the soul of culture. The core values of socialism are the concentrated expression of the contemporary Chinese spirit and the ideological and moral foundation that unites Chinese power. The 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China proposed to advocate prosperity, democracy, civilization and harmony, advocate freedom, equality, justice, and the rule of law, advocate patriotism, dedication, integrity, and friendliness, and actively cultivate and practice the core values of socialism. Xi Jinping pointed out that prosperity, democracy, civilization and harmony are the values of the national level. Freedom, equality, justice and the rule of law are the values of society. The values of patriotism, dedication, honesty and friendliness are the values of the citizen level. This summary actually answers the major questions of what kind of country we want to build, what kind of society to build, and what kind of citizenship to cultivate. Therefore, in the main theme of building a magnificent era of socialism with Chinese characteristics, these 24 words are the concentrated expression of the advanced socialist culture. Xi Jinping emphasized: "The cultural movements are in line with the national movement, and the context is connected with the national pulse." In the past five years, Xi Jinping has repeatedly emphasized the work of literature and art. In October 2014, Xi Jinping hosted a symposium on literary and art work and exchanged views with comrades in the literary and art circles to further clarify the direction and tasks of prospering and developing socialist literature and art under the new situation. In November 2016, Xi Jinping also attended the opening ceremony of the China National Literary Federation's Top Ten and the Chinese Writers' Association and delivered an important speech. Xi Jinping pointed out that the literary and artistic achievements of any era can only be issued with a vibrating voice if they are closely maintained and shared with the country and the nation. It is time to change, take advantage of the trend, and change at any time. Resonance with the times is the law of literature and art. Leaving the fiery social practice, standing outside the main theme of the grand era, muttering to himself, can only be eliminated by the times. Xi Jinping called on the vast number of literary and art workers to take the cultivation and promotion of the core values of socialism as their fundamental task, and unswervingly use the unique thoughts, emotions and aesthetics of the Chinese to create outstanding works of this era and with distinctive Chinese style. Only such works can reflect the voice of the times, show the people's struggle, inspire the national spirit, cultivate noble sentiments, show a brighter future for our people, and paint a brighter future for our nation.
