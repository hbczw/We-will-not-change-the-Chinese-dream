medical insurance

According to the report of the 19th National Congress, "People's health is an important symbol of national prosperity and national prosperity. We must deepen the reform of the medical and health system, comprehensively establish a basic medical and health system with Chinese characteristics, a medical security system, and a high-quality and efficient medical and health service system." Unified urban and rural residents' basic medical insurance system and major illness insurance system." The medical security system is an important part of promoting the construction of a healthy China. On the basis of popularizing basic medical insurance, China has built a major illness insurance covering all urban and rural residents. This policy has benefited more than one billion inhabitants and is highly recognized in the international community.

Number of medical insurance

According to the chart, the overall trend of the number of people participating in the rural medical aid cooperative medical project in 2012-2015 is gradually increasing, and the number of participants in urban medical assistance and medical insurance is increasing year by year. This shows that more and more people support the national medical security system.

The main content of China's medical security system

China's medical security system is based on basic medical insurance and urban and rural medical assistance. It also includes other forms of supplementary medical insurance and commercial health insurance. It mainly includes basic medical insurance for urban residents and new rural cooperative medical care.

Urban residents' basic medical insurance

The basic medical insurance for urban residents is an integral part of social medical insurance. It adopts the government-led, mainly individual (family) payment, and the government's appropriate subsidy supplements, according to the principle of consistent payment standards and treatment levels. Urban residents provide medical insurance systems for medical needs. The urban residents' medical insurance is a medical insurance system in which urban minors and unemployed residents who do not participate in urban workers' medical insurance are the main insured. It is a major measure for the Party Central Committee and the State Council to further solve the problem of medical security for the masses and continuously improve the medical security system following the implementation of the basic medical insurance system for urban workers and the implementation of the new rural cooperative medical system. It mainly makes institutional arrangements for urban non-employed residents' medical insurance. The emergence of this system is of great significance in the course of China's social insurance system reform, pointing out the direction of China's social insurance system reform.

New rural cooperative medical care

The new type of rural cooperative medical care (NRCMS) refers to the peasant medical mutual aid and mutual aid system that is organized, guided and supported by the government, and voluntarily participated by the peasants, and the individual, the collective and the government raise funds in a multi-disciplinary manner. It raises funds by means of personal contributions, collective support and government funding. In 2017, the per capita subsidy standard for the new rural cooperative medical system will increase by 30 yuan to 450 yuan on the basis of 2016. Among them, the central government will subsidize the newly added part according to the proportion of 80% in the western region and 60% in the central region. For certain provinces in the eastern region, they will be subsidized according to a certain percentage. The individual payment standard for farmers will increase by 30 yuan on the basis of 2016. In principle, the national average will reach 180 yuan. Explore the establishment of a stable and sustainable financing mechanism that is compatible with the level of economic and social development and the ability of all parties to withstand.
