Completing the building of a moderately prosperous society in all respects

Report of the 18th National Congress

The report of the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China first proposed a comprehensive "completed" society. The difference between “construction” and “construction” is far-reaching.

The "well-off society" was a strategic concept put forward by Deng Xiaoping in planning the blueprint for China's economic and social development in the late 1970s and early 1980s. With the deepening of the cause of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, its connotation and significance have been continuously enriched and developed. In the case of basically achieving a "well-off" at the end of the 20th century, the report of the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China clearly stated that "a well-off society should be built in an all-round way."

Nineteenth report

On October 18, 2017, Xi Jinping pointed out that we must not only build a well-off society in an all-round way, but also achieve the goal of the first hundred years of struggle, and we must take the opportunity to start a new journey of building a socialist modernized country in an all-round way and march toward the goal of the second century.

President Xi Jinping said that from now until 2002, it is the decisive period for building a well-off society in an all-round way. In accordance with the requirements of the 16th National Congress, the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, and the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, we must closely follow the major contradictions in our society and coordinate the promotion of economic construction, political construction, cultural construction, social construction, and ecological civilization. Implement the strategy of rejuvenating the country through science and education, the strategy of strengthening the country through talents, the strategy of innovation-driven development, the strategy of rural revitalization, the strategy of regional coordinated development, the strategy of sustainable development, the strategy of integration of military and civilian development, highlighting key points, complementing short-boards, strengths and weaknesses, especially resolutely It is a good to prevent and resolve major risks, precision poverty alleviation, and pollution prevention and control, so that the comprehensive well-off society can be recognized by the people and can withstand historical tests.

Winning a comprehensive well-off society

Winning a comprehensive well-off society

Two stages

President Xi proposed that a comprehensive analysis of the international and domestic situation and China's development conditions can be arranged in two phases from the year 2000 to the middle of this century.

  • In the first stage, from the 2nd year to the 2nd and 25th years, on the basis of building a well-off society in an all-round way, we will fight for another 15 years and basically realize socialist modernization. At that time, China's economic strength and scientific and technological strength will jump sharply and become the forefront of an innovative country; the people's equal participation and equal development rights are fully guaranteed, and the rule of law, the rule of law, and the rule of law are basically completed, and all aspects of the system are more perfect, and national governance The modernization of the system and governance capacity has been basically realized; the degree of social civilization has reached a new height, the national cultural soft power has been significantly enhanced, the influence of Chinese culture has become more extensive; the people's lives have become more affluent, the proportion of middle-income groups has increased significantly, and the urban-rural regional development gap and residents' lives The horizontal gap has been significantly reduced, the basic public service equalization has been basically realized, and the common prosperity of all the people has taken a solid step; the modern social governance pattern has basically taken shape, the society is full of vitality and harmony, and the ecological environment has basically improved, and the beautiful Chinese goal has basically been realized.

  • In the second stage, from the 23rd to the middle of this century, on the basis of basically realizing modernization, we will fight for another fifteen years and build China into a prosperous, strong, democratic, civilized, harmonious and beautiful socialist modernization power. By then, China's material civilization, political civilization, spiritual civilization, social civilization, and ecological civilization will be comprehensively upgraded, and the modernization of the national governance system and governance capacity will be achieved. It will become a country with comprehensive national strength and international influence, and the common prosperity of all people will be basically realized. Our people will enjoy a more happy and healthy life, and the Chinese nation will stand in the world's national forest with a more high-spirited attitude.

Four comprehensive

The "four comprehensive" theory shows that General Secretary Xi Jinping's overall framework for governing the country is more complete and mature. The "four comprehensive" complements each other, promotes each other, and complements each other. It not only includes strategic goals, but also includes the motivation to achieve strategic goals, lead norms, and fundamental guarantees.

Building a well-off society in an all-round way

Building a well-off society in an all-round way is the leader of the "four comprehensive". Building a well-off society in an all-round way is a strategic goal that takes the overall picture. From the Third Plenary Session of the 18th CPC Central Committee to launch comprehensive and deep reforms, to the Fourth Plenary Session of the 18th Central Committee to comprehensively promote the rule of law, the two plenary sessions, the two major themes, and two decisions, all reflect the party Central Committee with Xi Jinping as the general secretary. The strategic move to build a blueprint for a well-off society in an all-round way.

Comprehensively deepen reform

Comprehensively deepening reforms provides an inexhaustible motive force for building a well-off society in an all-round way. Facing the new situation and new tasks, building a well-off society in an all-round way, and building a strong, democratic, civilized and harmonious socialist modern country, and realizing the Chinese dream of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, we must comprehensively deepen reforms at the new historical starting point and continuously enhance socialism with Chinese characteristics. Road confidence, theoretical self-confidence, and institutional confidence.

Promote the rule of law in an all-round way

Comprehensively promoting the rule of law is the guiding and standardization of building a well-off society in an all-round way. To fully build a well-off society and realize the Chinese dream of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, to comprehensively deepen reform, improve and develop the socialist system with Chinese characteristics, and improve the party's ability to govern and the level of governance, we must comprehensively promote the rule of law.

Strictly rule the party

Comprehensively administering the party strictly is the fundamental guarantee for building a well-off society in an all-round way. It's up to The communist party to do thing well in China. The 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China made a comprehensive plan for strengthening the leadership of the party. The Third Session of the 18th Central Committee made arrangements for "strengthening and improving the party's leadership over comprehensive and deepening reforms", pointing out that the comprehensive deepening of reforms must strengthen and improve the party's Leading the party, giving full play to the party's overall role of leadership and overall coordination of the leadership.

Five indicators

The five indicators are based on the overall layout of the socialist five-in-one with Chinese characteristics, and have enriched and improved the goal of building a well-off society in an all-round way from the following five aspects, and measured a standard for the progress of building a well-off society in an all-round way.

  • economic development

    Enhance development coordination and strive to achieve sound and rapid economic development. Significant progress has been made in transforming the development mode. On the basis of optimizing the structure, improving efficiency, reducing consumption, and protecting the environment, the per capita GDP will quadruple by 2020 compared with 2000. The socialist market economic system is more complete. The capacity for independent innovation has increased remarkably, and the contribution rate of scientific and technological progress to economic growth has risen sharply, entering the ranks of innovative countries. The household consumption rate has steadily increased, forming a growth pattern driven by consumption, investment and exports. The coordinated development mechanism of urban and rural areas and regional and the layout of main functional areas have basically taken shape. Significant progress has been made in building a new socialist countryside.

  • Cultural Construction

    Strengthen cultural construction and significantly improve the quality of the nation's civilization. The socialist core value system is deeply rooted in the hearts of the people, and the good ideological and moral style is further promoted. The public cultural service system covering the whole society has been basically established. The cultural industry has significantly increased the proportion of the national economy, and the international competitiveness has been significantly enhanced. The cultural products that meet the needs of the people are more abundant.

  • Social construction

    Accelerate the development of social undertakings and comprehensively improve people's lives. The modern national education system is more perfect, the lifelong education system is basically formed, and the education level of the whole people and the level of training of innovative talents are significantly improved. Social employment is more adequate. The social security system covering urban and rural residents has been basically established, and everyone enjoys basic living security. A rational and orderly pattern of income distribution has basically taken shape, with middle-income people accounting for the majority and absolute poverty basically eliminated. Everyone has access to basic medical and health services. The social management system is more robust.

  • Political construction

    Expand socialist democracy and better protect people's rights and interests and social fairness and justice. Citizens’ political participation has expanded in an orderly manner. The basic strategy of governing the country according to law has been implemented in depth, the concept of the legal system of the whole society has been further strengthened, and the construction of the rule of law government has achieved new results. The grassroots democratic system is more perfect. The government’s ability to provide basic public services has increased significantly.

  • Construction of ecological civilization

    Building an ecological civilization will basically form an industrial structure, growth mode, and consumption pattern that conserve energy resources and protect the ecological environment. The circular economy has formed a large scale, and the proportion of renewable energy has increased significantly. The discharge of major pollutants was effectively controlled and the quality of the ecological environment was significantly improved. The concept of ecological civilization is firmly established in the whole society. Building a well-off society in an all-round way is the fundamental interest of the people of the country.
