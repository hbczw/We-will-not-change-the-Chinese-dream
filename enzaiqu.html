Wenchuan Earthquake-Stricken Area

The 2008 “5.12” Wenchuan Earthquake has passed for a full decade. The earthquake-stricken areas in Sichuan Province in the past decade have undergone earth-shaking changes. Wenchuan after the earthquake has already made remarkable achievements in urban planning and urban construction, and will have greater development potential in the future. Residents after the earthquake have also lived in a community with a comfortable environment and a harmonious atmosphere, enjoying a new life with complete security and worry-free living. After ten years, I will review the unforgettable earthquake in order to not forget the "natural disasters, the love of the world", and not forget the initial heart of "all the people, earthquake relief" under the leadership of the party. Sichuan is moving in the direction of realizing the great Chinese dream of the Chinese nation.

Ludian disaster area - Nirvana rebirth

Three years ago, the earth trembled, the landslide broke, and the house collapsed. A sudden earthquake caused 617 deaths, 112 people were missing, 3143 people were injured, 229,700 people were urgently resettled, and 1,084,400 people lost their ancestors. Home. In the past three years, in the once devastated ruins, the disaster-stricken areas have achieved great changes in living conditions, social undertakings, infrastructure improvements, and industrial restructuring. The broken homes of Nirvana are reborn and rejuvenated everywhere. After three years of restoration and reconstruction, the leading mountain town Nirvana was born again, and the mass production and living conditions spanned at least 30 years.All of them have been completed, and the reconstruction goals of “household settlement, home ownership, township improvement, ecological improvement, facility improvement, and economic development” have been basically achieved.

Reconstruction of the Lushan disaster area - full of hope for a better tomorrow

On April 20, 2013, a 7.0-magnitude earthquake occurred in Lushan. The road in Longmen Township was interrupted, the house collapsed, and the infrastructure was seriously damaged. A few years have passed, and now the Longmen Township, the new homes are rising, the roads are clean and tidy, and the tourism industry is booming. Longmen Ancient Town has become a national 4A-level scenic spot with “combination of agriculture and tourism, integration of towns and villages, and integration of villages and villages”. The rebuilding of the dream of the local people “staying in a good house, living a good life, developing good habits, and forming a good atmosphere” has gradually become a reality.Nowadays, Lushan people have lived in new houses and lived a good life. Lushan has successfully completed the first step of the post-disaster reconstruction goal of “three years of basic completion, five years of overall leapfrogging, and seven years of synchronized well-off”. The people of Lushan are grateful and confident in writing a better tomorrow.
