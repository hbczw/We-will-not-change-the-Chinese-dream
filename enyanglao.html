Pension service

For the first time, the 19th National Congress reported that the issue of old-age care is in line with employment, education, medical care, and housing. This has never happened in the past few years, which means that the issue of old-age care has risen to a national strategic level and will be given priority. .

China's existing pension policy issues

The goal of the pension policy is to build a corresponding pension system on the basis of certain social, economic, institutional and cultural aspects. Therefore, to establish the basic framework of China's pension policy, the general principle is: adhere to the sustainable development strategy and healthy aging development strategy, adapt to the social, economic and cultural conditions of the primary stage of socialism and the existing and possible comprehensive national strength level, According to the objective trend of population aging, the needs and willingness of people entering the old age in different historical periods, conforming to the overall requirements of the planned economy to the market economy in terms of institutional innovation, coordinating the development focus of state-owned enterprise reform, and attaching importance to the objective of rural pension Demand, fully consider the characteristics of traditional culture, play the role of various forms of old-age care, allocate pension resources as a whole, and establish a pension system that matches the overall development of China's social economy.

The basic framework of the pension policy includes:

  • Establish a basic old-age security policy system to ensure the basic life of the elderly in their later years.
  • Establish a medical insurance system to disperse the risk of disease risk in the elderly in later years.
  • Establish a social service policy for old-age care to solve the problem of care for the elderly.
  • Establish supporting policies to support family pension, stimulate the enthusiasm of family members to support the elderly, and make the elderly have a policy guarantee for home-based care.

Focus on Guangxi's pension measures

It is proposed to establish a comprehensive reform pilot zone for the national old-age service industry, and build a “one-core four-zone” structure in Guangxi to build Guangxi into a pilot program for the reform of the national old-age service industry.

Vigorously develop social welfare and charitable causes, especially the aged care service industry. In 2015, we tried to establish a “rural happy home” in rural areas.

During the “Twelfth Five-Year Plan” period, Guangxi increased its investment in civil infrastructure. According to preliminary statistics, the accumulated investment reached 2.868 billion yuan, an increase of 1.539 billion yuan over the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan", an increase of 1.16 times.

Nowadays, every city in Guangxi has a welfare institution or a pension institution, a welfare institution in each county, and a nursing home in each township. The number of old-age beds in Guangxi has increased from 100,000 at the end of the 11th Five-Year Plan to 161,000 at the end of the 12th Five-Year Plan. The average number of beds per 1,000 elderly people has reached 24.6.

There are a total of 7.82 million elderly people over the age of 60 in Guangxi, 1.15 million elderly people over 80 years old, and 5,024 elderly people in the 100-year-old. Therefore, Guangxi has entered the aging society earlier in the country, and pension has become a common concern of the society.

Vigorously develop community care for the elderly, and established more than 800 home care service centers or day care centers in Guangxi. It can be said that home care is from nothing to nothing. According to statistics, the coverage rate of community home care and rural community care services in Guangxi is now 61.2% and 30.3% respectively.
